**HR Prompt Library – 50+ Ready-to-Use Templates**

**1\. Attendance & Punctuality**

1. First Warning – Late Arrivals "Draft a professional yet firm first warning email to \[Employee Name\] for arriving late on \[Dates\] (total \[X\] instances this month). Reference company policy, state impact on team, set clear expectation, offer support, and schedule a follow-up meeting."

2. Final Warning – Chronic Lateness "Write a final written warning for \[Employee Name\] regarding persistent lateness. Mention previous warnings on \[Dates\], state that further violations may lead to termination, require acknowledgment signature."

3. Warning – Unauthorized Absences "Create a warning notice for \[Employee Name\] who was absent on \[Dates\] without prior approval or notification."

#### **2\. Performance Issues**

4. PIP Notice (Performance Improvement Plan) "Generate a 30-60-90 day PIP document for \[Employee Name\], \[Role\], currently failing to meet \[Specific KPI\]. Include measurable goals, support provided, weekly check-ins, and consequences of non-improvement."

5. Low Productivity – Gentle Nudge "Write a supportive but direct email to \[Employee Name\] whose output has dropped \[X\]% in the last \[Y\] weeks. Express concern, ask if anything is affecting performance, and set a 1:1 meeting."

#### **3\. Policy Violations**

6. Dress Code Violation "Draft a polite email reminding \[Employee Name\] of the company dress code after wearing \[inappropriate attire\] on \[Date\]."

7. Social Media Policy Breach "Write a warning letter to \[Employee Name\] who posted \[describe post briefly\] violating our social media policy."

8. Data Security / Confidentiality Breach "Create a serious warning notice for sharing confidential information via \[channel\] on \[Date\]."

#### **4\. Positive / Recognition**

9. Appreciation Email After Project Success "Write a warm thank-you email from manager/HR to \[Employee Name\] for outstanding contribution to \[Project Name\]."

10. Promotion Announcement (Internal) "Draft an internal announcement for \[Employee Name\]’s promotion from \[Old Role\] to \[New Role\], effective \[Date\]. Highlight key achievements."

####   **5\. Hiring & Offers**

11. Job Offer Email – Standard "Generate a professional job offer email for \[Candidate Name\], \[Role\] at \[Salary\] LPA/CTC, joining date \[Date\], with standard benefits and next steps."

12. Rejection Email – Post Interview "Write a kind and encouraging rejection email to a candidate who reached final round but was not selected."

#### **6\. Onboarding & Exit**

13. Day-1 Welcome Email to New Joiner "Create a warm welcome email for \[New Employee Name\] joining on \[Date\] as \[Role\]. Include reporting time, documents required, and buddy name."

14. Exit Interview Questions (10 Best) "List the 10 most effective exit interview questions that reveal real reasons for leaving."

15. Clearance & Full-and-Final Settlement Email "Draft a FnF settlement email for \[Employee Name\], last working day \[Date\], detailing dues, experience letter, and relieving date."

#### **7\. Leaves & WFH**

16. Leave Application Rejection (No Balance) "Politely reject \[Employee Name\]’s leave request from \[Dates\] due to zero leave balance and high workload period. Suggest alternatives."

17. Return-to-Office Notice (Hybrid Mandate) "Write a clear company-wide email mandating \[X\] days/week in office starting \[Date\], with reasons and support offered."

#### **8\. Conflict & Sensitive Issues**

18. Warning – Unprofessional Behavior in Meeting "Draft a warning email to \[Employee Name\] for raising voice/interrupting repeatedly in \[Date\] team meeting."

19. Handling Romantic Relationship at Workplace "Create a professional email requesting \[Employee A\] and \[Employee B\] to disclose their relationship and sign a consensual relationship agreement."

20. Response to Harassment Complaint Acknowledgment "Write an immediate acknowledgment email to complainant assuring confidentiality, no retaliation, and investigation timeline."

####     **Bonus Quick-Fire Templates (Copy-Paste Ready)** 

21. Birthday Wish (Official) "Wish \[Employee Name\] a very happy birthday on behalf of \[Company Name\] and the team."

22. Work Anniversary Congrats "Congratulate \[Employee Name\] on completing \[X\] years with \[Company Name\]."

23. Meeting Reminder – Disciplinary Discussion "Send a formal meeting invite to \[Employee Name\] for discussion regarding performance/conduct on \[Date & Time\]."
